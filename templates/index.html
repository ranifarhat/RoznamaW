Reviews

"This application of time management and mental wellbeing is highly needed among adolescents. It is a combination of a friend and a mentor, the way it is presented by an application, is the most popular method of reaching adolescents. Currently, it seems the application has much more room to grow, even with that, the future of the application seems promising, given it will be continuously customized and personalized according to the users' preferences. Hopefully, it will be like Waze or a GPS for the users, guiding them to the path of success." - CEO of a private hospital and a neurolinguistic programming master practitioner.

"This app is important, especially for teenagers. It offers psycho-education to those who need it most, as well as to those who are typically most vulnerable to difficulties with time management and understanding specific emotions and the impact they may have on wellbeing and performance. If a person, specifically teenagers, if they make goals and plans, and go through with those plans and achieve their goals; it will increase self-esteem and increase performance. Hence leading the person to success. The application appears to have potential if the right steps are taken. I imagine it would be easier to follow than a normal plain schedule, as a more personal aspect is put into the mix, causing the user to feel more determined to follow through with their plans and tasks." - Head of a Psychiatric Medical ward.
